About Us

More than 25 years turning spaces into experiences.

Founded in 1998 by Emerson Perlagides, Official Brasil is a national reference in promotional architecture, scenography, shows, stands and corporate events. With commercial headquarters in Guarulhos-SP and a production facility in Arujá-SP, we deliver projects across Brazil.

Our team delivers complete solutions:

  • Shows — stages, lighting, structure and tents.
  • Customized projects in 3 days and edits in 1 business day.
  • Tradeshows, stands, scenography and exhibition assembly.
  • Catering, furniture, equipment and event promotion.
